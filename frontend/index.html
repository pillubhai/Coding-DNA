Key Features

LLM-Powered Agents

AI agents use Large Language Models to make intelligent suppression decisions in real-time.

Dynamic Fire Spread

Realistic fire dynamics with wind, spot fires, moisture, and fuel-based propagation.

Live Analytics

Real-time charts, score tracking, and performance metrics for every simulation run.

Multi-Unit Control

Command 1 Air Tanker and 2 Ground Crews with coordinated suppression strategies.

Adjustable Difficulty

Three difficulty levels: Easy (5 structures), Medium (10), Hard (20+ structures).

Weather System

Dynamic wind direction and speed affects fire spread and suppression effectiveness.
